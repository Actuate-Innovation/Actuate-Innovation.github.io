About Actuate

Actuate is a new kind of nonprofit organization, formed to conduct solutions R&D for society’s critical problems. We run programs that create and demonstrate breakthroughs - and model a powerful new form of innovation for these challenges.

Actuate was founded in 2019. During our seed stage, we’re defining our first programs, fundraising, and building partnerships for the work ahead.
